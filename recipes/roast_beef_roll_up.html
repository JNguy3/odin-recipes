Roast Beef Horseradish Roll-Ups

Description

The Roast Beef Horseradish Hand Wrap is a delectable fusion of robust flavors and convenient portability, making it a perfect option for a quick lunch or a satisfying snack. This wrap features tender slices of roast beef, known for their rich and savory taste, paired with a zesty horseradish sauce that adds a sharp, spicy kick. The horseradish not only complements the beef's depth of flavor but also brings a refreshing contrast that tantalizes the taste buds. Fresh, crisp vegetables like lettuce, tomatoes, and red onions are added for crunch and balance, while a soft tortilla or flatbread encases all the ingredients, making it easy to enjoy on the go.

What sets the Roast Beef Horseradish Hand Wrap apart is its versatility and appeal to a wide range of palates. It can be customized with additional ingredients such as cheese, pickles, or even a touch of mustard for extra flavor. Whether you're packing it for a picnic, a work lunch, or a road trip, this wrap maintains its texture and taste, providing a satisfying meal without the fuss. The combination of high-quality roast beef and the bold, pungent flavor of horseradish ensures that each bite is both hearty and invigorating, making this hand wrap a standout choice for meat lovers and sandwich enthusiasts alike.

Ingredients

  • Prepared Horseradish
  • Dijon-style Mustard
  • Fish Sauce
  • Flour Tortilla
  • Spinach Leaves
  • Cooked Deli Roast Beef
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Steps

Directions:

    Step 1:

  1. Beat the cream cheese, horseradish, and mustard together in a bowl until well blended.

    2. Step 2:

  2. Spread a thin layer of the cream cheese mixture over each tortilla. Arrange spinach leaves evenly over the tortillas. Place two slices of roast beef over the cream cheese. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese, dividing evenly between tortillas. Starting at one end, gently roll up each tortilla into a tight tube. Wrap with aluminum foil or plastic wrap to keep the rolls tight. Refrigerate at least 4 hours.

    3. Step 3:

  3. To serve for lunch, unwrap and slice into 2 or 3 pieces. Only cut the rolls you will be using that day so the others do not dry out. To serve for parties, unwrap and slice the rolls diagonally into 1 inch sections, and arrange on a serving platter.
